How to Use Comparium

Quick Start Guide

  1. Select Fish Species: Click on a category to expand it, then click on a fish species to select it. You must select at least 2 fish to compare.
  2. Use Search: Type in the search box to quickly find fish by common or scientific name.
  3. Compare: Click the "Compare Species" button to see detailed compatibility analysis.
  4. Review Results: Check temperature, pH, tank size requirements, and compatibility warnings.

Understanding the Results

What We Compare:

  • Temperature Range: Fish need overlapping temperature requirements to thrive together
  • pH Level: Water acidity/alkalinity must be compatible
  • Water Hardness: Soft vs hard water preferences
  • Tank Size: Minimum gallons needed for all selected species
  • Adult Size: How large each fish will grow
  • Temperament: Peaceful, semi-aggressive, or aggressive behavior
  • Diet: Omnivore, carnivore, or herbivore
  • Social Needs: Schooling requirements and group sizes
  • Lifespan: Expected years with proper care
  • Care Level: Difficulty from "Very Easy" to "Difficult"

Compatibility Warnings Explained

Critical Issues:

  • No Temperature Overlap: Fish require incompatible water temperatures
  • No pH Overlap: Water chemistry is incompatible
  • Predation Risk: Larger fish will eat smaller tankmates or invertebrates
  • Aggressive Combinations: Species known to fight or cause injury

Warnings:

  • Narrow Overlaps: Temperature or pH ranges are compatible but tight
  • Size Mismatches: Large fish might prey on smaller fish if hungry
  • Fin Nipping: Some species harass long-finned tankmates
  • Territorial Behavior: Semi-aggressive fish need hiding spots and space

Good Signs:

  • Compatible Parameters: Temperature and pH ranges overlap well
  • Peaceful Community: All species have peaceful temperaments
  • Appropriate Tank Size: Minimum gallons needed for healthy living

Database Information

Current Species Count: 143 freshwater aquarium species

Data Sources: Information averaged from reputable aquarium sources including:

  • SeriouslyFish.com
  • Aquarium Co-Op
  • FishLore
  • Aqueon
  • FishBase
  • PlanetCatfish (for catfish species)

Important Note

Comparium is a planning tool, not a guarantee. Always research each species thoroughly from multiple sources, consult with experienced aquarists, and monitor your tank closely when introducing new species.
